OpenTyrian 2000 modified to work on the A30 with Center 2x scaling. 

OpenTyrian2000 is an open-source port of the DOS game Tyrian. It is a fork of OpenTyrian, 
with the end goal being to replicate the experience of Tyrian 2000 as closely as possible.
Tyrian is an arcade-style vertical scrolling shooter. 
The story is set in 20,031 where you play as Trent Hawkins, a skilled fighter-pilot employed to fight MicroSol and save the galaxy.

Port by mettalic77, December 2024
